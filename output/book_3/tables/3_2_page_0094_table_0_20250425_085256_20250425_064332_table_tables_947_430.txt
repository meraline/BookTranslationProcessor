Table content: The best way to actually enumerate would be to use Cardrunners EV
and plug in the situation and ranges given an action. In order to figure out
which action is best, you would then create another tree given the other
possible action, different opponent actions and different ranges. Aside
from sitting down and spending a few hours doing that, you could either
sit down with a piece of paper or excel spreadsheet and plug numbers and
strings of calculations together, or just generalize to get an idea of what
will happen. I think using an excel spreadsheet is the best choice and
the most “hands on” solution, even though it’s less accurate, but all three
options have their benefits based on how much time you have and the
complexity of the situation. Let’s take a look at the three steps you should

take when seeking to enumerate out a hand.
